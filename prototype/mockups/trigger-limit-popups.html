Why we gently stop here

This isn’t a character limit for its own sake. It’s a guardrail for cue specificity — the property that makes deliberate rehearsal stick when the real moment arrives.

  • 01A brief phrase is easier to retrieve under arousal than a paragraph of context.
  • 02Specificity without elaboration strengthens the stimulus→response link you’re replacing.
  • 03What you can hold in one glance is what you can summon on purpose.
