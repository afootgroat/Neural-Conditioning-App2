How does Rewire begin?

Three onboarding philosophies. Each is a living phone — tap through every frame. Same brand. Same voice. Different ways to teach the practice without becoming a tutorial.

Direction A

Arrival

Brand first. One breath with a living orb. Almost no words — the app introduces itself the way a meditation object would: by being held.

Direction B

The Ritual

Teach Notice → Accept → Choose by living it once. Heat cools to violet. One guided rep before any pathway exists — muscle memory before vocabulary.

Trigger → Notice → Accept → Choose → Fire
Direction C

First Thread

Intimate second-person. Three quiet questions that become your first pathway. Onboarding dissolves into the wizard — no seam between learning and doing.

Direction D

Arrival · short

A’s brand, practice, and begin — breath removed. Three beats from mark to naming a pattern.
